## Caption

5 signs you're overtraining — and what to do about each one.

More training doesn't always mean better results. Sometimes the bravest thing you can do is take a rest day.

Here's what your body is trying to tell you:

1. Your easy pace suddenly feels hard
2. You're exhausted but can't sleep
3. Every small niggle turns into an injury
4. You dread sessions you used to love
5. Your times are going backwards despite more volume

The fix isn't always "push through." Sometimes it's a deload week. Sometimes it's 3 days off. Sometimes it's admitting the plan needs adjusting.

A good training plan builds recovery in. If yours doesn't — that's the problem.

Save this for the next time you're wondering whether to push through or pull back.
