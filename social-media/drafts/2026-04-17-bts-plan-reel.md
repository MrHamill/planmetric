## Caption

Here's what actually goes into a personalised training plan.

It's not a template with your name pasted at the top. It's not a generic 12-week block pulled off a shelf.

Every plan starts with 50+ data points from your intake form — race date, goal time, injury history, weekly hours, experience level, even which days you prefer to train.

From there: custom training zones, a week-by-week structure built around YOUR life, and coaching notes that explain the why behind every session.

The plan runs from purchase date to race date. No preset lengths. Your timeline. Your training.

That's the difference between a plan and a template.
