## Filming Instructions

### Concept

Split-screen or quick-cut format. "Expectation" shows the slick, pro-level version. "Reality" shows the chaotic, relatable disaster. Film both versions of each transition moment.

### Shot List

| # | Duration | Shot | Expectation | Reality |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 0–3s | **HOOK** | — | Text: "Triathlete transition area: expectation vs reality" |
| 2 | 3–8s | **Racking the bike** | Smooth, one-handed rack. Helmet already on. Cool walk away. | Fumbling the bike onto the rack. It falls. You catch it. Nearly knock over the bike next to you. |
| 3 | 8–13s | **Finding your spot** | Walk straight to your perfectly laid-out towel with gear in neat rows. | Wandering around confused. "Was it this rack?" Looking at other people's stuff. Counting racks. |
| 4 | 13–18s | **Wetsuit removal** | Unzip, peel, step out in one fluid motion. | Hopping on one leg, wetsuit stuck on ankle. Sitting down. Using the other foot. Grunting. Still stuck. |
| 5 | 18–23s | **Putting on shoes** | Shoes pre-clipped on bike. Elastic laces. 2 seconds flat. | Wrong shoe on wrong foot. Laces tangled. Sand everywhere. One shoe goes flying. |
| 6 | 23–28s | **Running out of T2** | Sprint out of transition. Smooth. Fast. Looking fresh. | Jelly legs. Running like a newborn giraffe. Nearly tripping over a race belt. |
| 7 | 28–33s | **The time check** | Glance at watch. Nod. Exactly as planned. | Look at watch. Visible shock. "How was I in there for 4 minutes?!" |
| 8 | 33–38s | **End card** | — | Text: "If your T2 is under 3 minutes, you're lying" + @planmetric |

### Camera & Angles

- **Setup location:** Garage, backyard, or actual transition area at a local race/training session
- **Expectation shots:** Tripod, clean angle, steady. Film in good light. Make it look polished.
- **Reality shots:** Slightly chaotic handheld is fine — adds to the comedy. Exaggerate the fumbling.
- **Framing:** Keep consistent camera position between expectation/reality pairs so the contrast hits harder
- **Wardrobe:** Full tri kit for both. Wetsuit for T1 shots. The more authentic the setup, the funnier it is.

### Text Overlays

1. **0–3s:** "Triathlete transition area" (top) + "Expectation vs Reality" (large, centred)
2. **3–8s:** "EXPECTATION" (clean, white) → cut → "REALITY" (shaky/messy font)
3. Repeat "EXPECTATION" / "REALITY" labels for each pair
4. **28–33s:** Optional — add a fake timer counting up way too fast during the "reality" time check
5. **33–38s:** "If your T2 is under 3 minutes, you're lying" + @planmetric
